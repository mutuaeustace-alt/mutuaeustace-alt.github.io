Who We Are

Founded in the heart of Nairobi in 2020, Solivage Travels is a boutique Kenyan travel company redefining adventure with a touch of soulful serenity. We believe travel isn't just about destinations—it's about the stories you carry home, the connections you forge, and the quiet moments that recharge your spirit. As proud stewards of Kenya's wild beauty, we craft bespoke experiences that blend adrenaline, culture, and conservation, ensuring every trip leaves the world a little better than we found it.

Our Mission

At Solivage, we're on a mission to make meaningful travel accessible to all, whether you're a solo wanderer chasing sunrises over the Maasai Mara, a family seeking hidden beaches on Diani's shores, or a corporate group bonding on a Serengeti safari. We prioritize sustainable practices: partnering with local communities, supporting eco-lodges, and offsetting carbon footprints so your voyage honors the land that hosts it.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Curated Kenyan Escapes: From Mount Kenya treks to Lamu archipelago cruises, our itineraries are 100% tailored—no cookie-cutter tours here.
  • Sustainable Soul: We give back 10% of profits to fund community wildlife projects and women's artisan cooperatives.
  • Personal Touch: Your dedicated travel curator (that's us!) handles everything, from visa tips to surprise cultural immersions.
